Mohegan Sun Introduction

Mohegan Sun, located in Uncasville, Connecticut, is one of the largest casinos in the United States. With the help of sound, lighting, and special effects the casino transports visitors to an ancient world. The casino contains slot machines, gaming tables including poker, blackjack, craps, roulette, Caribbean stud poker, keno and baccarat. They also offer video game blackjack, craps, and roulette. The race book offers live horse or greyhound racing.

Mohegan Sun offers a child-care center to entertain children so allowing parents to go off for their own entertainment. Come enjoy gaming and shopping in a historical setting. Take a seat and test your luck at over 325 tables featuring all your favorite games. Watch live music, entertainment, top headlining concerts, and premier sporting events in the three entertainment venues including a 10,000-seat Arena; a 350-seat Cabaret Theatre and a 300-seat Wolf Den.

People from across the globe come to see live concerts major sporting events including Mohegan Sun's Women's National Basketball Association team, the Connecticut Sun. There are a 20,000 square-foot world-class spa by Elemis and 10,000 square-foot indoor pool.
