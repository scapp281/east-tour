Orlando Introduction

Orlando is a large city located in Orange County, Florida. Orlando, for most people, conjures up the image of theme parks, mainly Walt Disney World. But Walt Disney World is not in Orlando, instead it is in Lake Buena Vista. Orlando and the surrounding areas have major international parks, smaller attractions, and an incredible family-entertainment orientation. Orlando is the place every kid dreams of visiting, and it has something for everyone in the family to enjoy.

Orlando is famous for being the theme park capital of America. Islands of Adventure, SeaWorld, and Universal Studios Florida are all in Orlando. Each of those attractions can be further broken down into numerous smaller parks and locations. For example, Disney World could breaks down into the Pleasure Island, MGM Studios, Magic Kingdom, Camp Disney, Epcot Center, and several others themed areas. The other parks offer everything from water rides and science exhibits to movie making magic. One of the best theme parks is Disney's Animal Kingdom. he park is home to about 1,500 animals representing 250 species.

Other than theme parks and attractions, Orlando also features other types of enjoyment such as world class golf courses, tennis clinics, spas, resorts. Orlando also features dozens of smaller attractions, and an enviable nightlife. Each of the theme parks and attractions will tempt you to enjoy their night time entertainment. There are local restaurants and clubs around that offer just as much fun. Orlando is home to hundreds of restaurants of every type to caters to all tastes to satisfy all international visitors.

It's hard to believe that Walt Disney World is 40 years old with all these new facilities. Orlando has been hosting millions of visitors a year for decades, so it has lots of experience in how to treat guests and make them feel welcome and deeply satisfying. Orlando is a city for everyone, especially families to enjoy live entertainment throughout the park. Take pictures and interact with your favorite Disney characters.
